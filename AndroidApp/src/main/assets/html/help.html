Zoo Mania is a rapid animal matching game in which you have to match the maximum possible animals before the timer runs down to zero. There will be four animals with four options displayed. You have to drag and drop the animal name on the picture of the animal. As soon as you match the name against the correct animal the next four animals will be displayed.

Category: Birds

When you select "Birds" category, pictures of birds will be displayed. There are 45 pictures of the birds in this category. You have 1 minute to match as many pictures as you can.

Category: Mammals

When you select "Mammals" category, pictures of mammals will be displayed. There are 45 pictures of the mammals in this category. You have 1 minute to match as many pictures as you can.
